How to Use ET 📖
1

Login with your mobile number

Enter your phone, name, age & gender. Get verified via OTP. Your data stays on your device only.

2

Add Emergency Contacts

Go to Contacts tab → Add up to 3 trusted people (family, friends). They'll be alerted the moment you press SOS.

3

Press & Hold SOS in an Emergency

On the SOS page, hold the red button for 3 seconds. Confirms, then instantly alerts all contacts with your live GPS link and calls 999.

4

Send SOS SMS instantly

Tap "Send SOS SMS" from home or contacts. Opens your SMS app pre-filled with your name + live location: "I am at [location], help me!"

5

Find Nearest Police Station

Tap "Police Near Me" for a live GPS-based list of nearest thana with direct call and directions buttons.
